Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

BaseMapped LLC (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the BaseCalc HVAC mobile application (“BaseCalc HVAC” or the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information Stored by the App

BaseCalc HVAC stores the job contacts, worksheets, calculations, and company settings you enter in the App's storage on your device. The App does not provide a BaseCalc account or send this user-entered business data to a BaseCalc cloud database. Operating-system backup behavior can depend on your platform and device settings; Android cloud backup is disabled for the App.

  • Job contact records — names, companies, phone numbers, email addresses, addresses, and notes you choose to enter.
  • Job worksheets and company details — work items, quantities, job state, notes, and the business information that appears on your worksheets.
  • Calculation history — inputs and results from HVAC calculations.

2. Purchases and Subscription Status

BaseCalc HVAC offers optional paid subscriptions for Pro access. Payments are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. RevenueCat manages subscription entitlement status and may process an app-generated user identifier, device and platform information, and store transaction or entitlement information needed to provide that service. We do not receive or store your payment card information. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details about its practices.

3. Advertising

This release does not include a third-party advertising SDK and does not display third-party ads.

4. PDF Export & Sharing

When you generate a job-worksheet PDF, it is created in the App's local storage. It is shared only when you explicitly use the system share sheet to send it to email, messaging, SpeakSheet, or another app. Copies received by another app are controlled by that app.

5. Data Sharing & Sale

We do not sell the user-entered business data stored by BaseCalc HVAC. The App does not send your job contacts, worksheets, company settings, or calculation history to a BaseCalc cloud database. Subscription service data is processed by the app store and RevenueCat as described above, and worksheet data leaves the App when you choose to share it.

6. Your Choices & Data Deletion

You can delete job contacts, job worksheets, and calculation-history entries directly in the App. Deleting a job contact also deletes its associated worksheets and calculations. Deleting a worksheet removes its generated in-app PDF when that file is still available to the App. Copies previously shared to another app are not deleted. Uninstalling the App removes its current app container from the device, but copies retained by another app or an operating-system backup must be managed through that service.

7. Children's Privacy

BaseCalc HVAC is a professional tool intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
