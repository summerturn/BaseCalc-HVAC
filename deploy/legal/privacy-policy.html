Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

BaseMapped LLC (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the BaseCalc HVAC mobile application (“BaseCalc HVAC” or the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

BaseCalc HVAC stores your job contacts, field tickets, calculations, and company settings locally on your device. No BaseCalc account or cloud sync is available, and the App does not send this user-entered business data to a BaseCalc cloud database.

  • Job contact records — names, companies, phone numbers, email addresses, addresses, and notes you choose to enter.
  • Field tickets and company details — workup items, estimated totals, job state, notes, and the business information that appears on your field tickets.
  • Calculation history — inputs and results from HVAC calculations.

2. Advertising & Identifiers

This release does not require personalized ad tracking. If we enable advertising in a future release, we will update this policy and the related store privacy disclosures to match the live behavior of the app.

3. Purchases

BaseCalc HVAC offers optional paid subscriptions for Pro access. Payments are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play, and subscription entitlement status is managed through RevenueCat so the App can unlock Pro features. We do not receive or store your payment card information.

4. PDF Export & Sharing

When you generate a field-ticket PDF, it is created on your device. It is only shared if you explicitly choose to share it via email, messaging, SpeakSheet, or another app. You control who receives it.

5. Data Sharing & Sale

We do not sell your personal information. BaseCalc HVAC does not share your job contacts, field tickets, company settings, or calculation history with a BaseCalc cloud database.

6. Your Choices & Data Deletion

You can delete any job contact, field ticket, or calculation directly in the App. Removing the App from your device deletes locally stored BaseCalc HVAC data from that device.

7. Children's Privacy

BaseCalc HVAC is a professional tool intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
