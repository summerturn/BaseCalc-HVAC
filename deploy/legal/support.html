BaseCalc HVAC Support

HVAC calculators, materials planning, and field-ticket records built for the job site.

Getting started

Open the Calculators tab and pick one of 18 field tools — BTU ↔ Tons, CFM from BTU, BTU from CFM, Duct Sizing, Air Velocity, Psychrometrics, Refrigerant Lines, Superheat / Subcooling, Room Load, Heat Pump Balance, Hydronics, Mixed Air, Air Changes, Evaporative Cooling, Filter Velocity, Combustion Analysis, Refrigerant Weight, or Economizer. Enter your values and tap Calculate to see a sizing guide or pass/fail result with the full breakdown. Every calculation is saved to History.

Jobs & field tickets

Use the Jobs tab to save job contacts and field tickets with workup items, site notes, and a field-ticket PDF. Use Materials for common pull-list reminders tied to the HVAC workflow.

SpeakSheet handoff

BaseCalc HVAC is not the final billing system. When a job is ready to bill, share the field-ticket summary into SpeakSheet and create the customer invoice there.

Is my data private?

Yes. Core calculators and locally saved records work offline. Job contacts, worksheets, settings, and calculation history use the App's storage on your device; BaseCalc does not provide cloud sync for that user-entered data. Subscription checks and any share action require the related external service. See our Privacy Policy for details.

A note on accuracy

BaseCalc HVAC is a professional reference tool. Always verify results against current industry standards, manufacturer data, and local codes. The authority having jurisdiction has the final say.
